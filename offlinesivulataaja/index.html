Webpage downloader (you can use them offline)

Step 1: Put on your bookmarkbar

Step 2: Drag link below to your bookmark bar

Offlinewebdownload

Step 3: Go to website you want to download and click the Offlinewebdownload-bookmark

*Input and button appears in top left corner of the site you currently are*

Step 4: Name the file to the input and then click download button

Step 5: Go to your computers Downloads and doubleclick the file, then it opens.
